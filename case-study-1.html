The Family Circle Cup

The Family Circle Cup, one of twenty premier events on the Sony Ericsson WTA Tour, has a roster of past champions that include some of the biggest names in the history of women’s tennis. The facility also hosts a variety of events throughout the year including concerts, festivals, tournaments & other special activities.
